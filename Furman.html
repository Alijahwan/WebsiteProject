F U R M A N

Furman is a private university located in Greenville South Carolina with a total undergraduate enrollment of 2,775 students. Furman focuses primarily on liberal arts and science education majors. Furman holds many high ranking awards including top 5 most innovative schools, third ranked for best college for veterans, and number 4 for most politically active students. Furman University also has one of the lowest teacher to student ratios, being 11:1, averaging around 13 students per classroom.

With over 65% of students participating in intramural sports and groups, Furman sports can also be quite competitive. As of 2019, Furman has over 20 Division 1 athletic teams with over 6000 square footage of training facilities and centers. With over 60+ areas of studies, Furman also has 150+ clubs and organizations for students to participate in.

In regards to the classroom experience, Furman incorporates research, internships, and community-centered learning into nearly every classroom which allows for a hands-on approach to learning. With smaller classroom sizes, students are given the ability to communicate more with professors and obtain additional assistance. Furman students closely work researchers at the David E. Shi Center and the Institute for the Advancement of Community Health. With over 5 different library locations, Furman is also ranked top in the nation for student satisfaction for research at their libraries.
