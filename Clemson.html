C L E M S O N



Clemson is a public University located in South Carolina with just over 20,000 students enrolled. Clemson was founded in 1889 and is best known for their engineering program and football team. The school itself is quite diverse, being split between 12,506 male and 11,881 female students across all demographics. Along with being the second largest university in the state, they rank in the top 100 universities nationally. Clemson also takes research very seriously and students often colloborate with staff members and industry members to improve enviornmental sustainability.

Moving past the rankings, Clemson offers plenty more than educational benefits. They offer a multitude of diversity programs including Cultural Exchange Community, Global Engagement, International Festival, and Multicultural programs. Additionally, there are plenty of club opportunities along with recreational activities offered on campus. This includes Intrumural Sports, Clemson Outdoor Recreation and Education (CORE), Center for Visual Arts, and a host of other clubs. Unfortunately, Clemson does not offer many academic scholarships, many come from athletics and sports such as football. However, Clemson does have a variety of housing opportunities, especially for first year students. They offer both residence halls and suites with the option of living in local apartments which are relatively close to campus. Additionally, there are a great deal of fraternities and Sororities for students to pick from, making housing at Clemson very enjoyable.
