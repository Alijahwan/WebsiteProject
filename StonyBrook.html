S T O N Y B R O O K


Founded in 1957, Stony Brook is a diverse University located in the State of New York with over 15,000 students. Stony Brook is recognized nationally as a top-tier research institution and an active member of the medical science and engineering field.

The most popular majors at Stony Brook are Health and Biomedical Sciences, Applied Mathematics, Computer Science, Business Management, Marketing, Engineering, and Psychology. With over 60 majors and 80 minors, Stony Brook ranked #91 nationally in the 2020 edition of best colleges and top 50 in Most Innovative Public Schools. Stony Brook also provides a multitude of scholarship opportunities for undergraduate students.

In addition the academics, Stony Brook has over 400 student-led clubs and organizations along with 30+ fraternities & sororities. This includes, but is not limited to: Marching Band, Earth Stock, Active Minds, ROTC, Culinary Club, Creative Writing and Film, and hundreds more. Stony Brook also encompasses the Staller Center for the Arts, which is a program offering theater, classical music and popular campus entertainment. Stony Brook also devotes a large portion of their resources towards their athletic departments and has multiple Division 1 Athletic sport teams. Housing and Campus Life at Stony Brook is very affordable with over 30 Residence Halls and 23 Apartment-Styled Buildings to choose from. As a result, over 10,300 students make the choice to live on campus. Needless to say, Stony Brook is a highly rated university with plenty of opportunities for nearly anyone in any academic field.
